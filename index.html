Trading Plants

House Plant Trading

There are different variety of plants that you could choose to trade, and houseplants are one of there. If you have a dream plant you don't neccessarily have to go out and buy it. Some people in the plant keeping community are willing to trade or even give away their beloved plants for different reasons, so keep on the look-out

Crop Trading

Another plant type some look to trade are crops. These edible plants can be keep your grocery bill lower by growing you own tomatoes or peppers to name a couple. Similar to houseplants there are people that just have too many of one type of crop and might just want to try growing a crop that you have and are willing to give away.

Seed Trading

Seed trades also occur pretty often. Seeds can grow into a crop or also a houseplant for decoration. This option is usually reserved for more experienced planters, due to the higher difficulty going through the different stages of a plants life. So depending on what exactly you are looking for you could get exactly what you're looking for while also making a spot for your new plant friend.
